Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take? +

Most repairs are completed within 2-4 hours. Complex issues may take 24-48 hours. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer home service? +

Yes! We offer doorstep service within Dhaka. Additional charges may apply based on your location.

What brands do you repair? +

We repair all major brands including Samsung, Apple, Xiaomi, Huawei, Oppo, Vivo, Sony, LG, Dell, HP, Lenovo, and more.

Is my data safe during repair? +

We take data privacy seriously. However, we recommend backing up your data before repair. We are not responsible for existing data loss.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, bKash, Nagad, bank transfers, and all major credit/debit cards. EMI options available for purchases.
